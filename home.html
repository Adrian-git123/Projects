A word from the Chief Principal

Mr. Maiyo Kipchumba

"We have been receiving many students from different parts of Kenya and even outside Kenya. One noticable fact that I had observed over the past 16 years of my regime as the principal of this prestigious institution is that these students are very much determined and are more focused towards the goal. What makes Kapsabet different from all the other schools is that we are focused like a Lion in the Jungle since we are Lions. Our determination and prayers lift us beyond the discovered and we do trust that through this we are going to make it."
